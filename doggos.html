  • Welcome to our dog rescue and adoption organization.
    We help and foster abandoned dogs in our region and through this site we help them get adopted.
    These heroes are looking for their forever homes and they can't wait to meet you!
    Every pup has its own pictures and a little description of itself.
    All you have to do is click on Adopt! button under the picture of the doggo you want and fill out the form.
    Why shop when you can adopt?

Mila

Mila is our youngest member. She's just 2 months old, but won't grow to a big size dog. She is very playful and active, but just as any baby - she gets tired after a few minutes of playing.

Max

Max is Corgi breed dog, fully grown. He's about 4 years old and weights 10kg. He doesn't shed very much, but he loves outdoors so a house with a backyard would be a great home for him.

Charlie

Charlie is another baby. He's 3 and a half months old,a bit shy but when you gain his trust he loves to play. He's a black labrador breed so he will grow up to be a big dog.

Lucy

Lucy is 2 years old Staford breed dog. People have a wrong idea of this dog breed, but we garantee you she is as calm and cuddly as any other puppy. She loves to play outside, but also loves indoors.

Bailey

Bailey is our oldest member. She is about 8 years old,so she's at her full size and all grown up. Shs'a a Corgi and German Shepard mix. She isn't very active but loves to cuddle,so if you're a couch potato she's perfect for you.

Jamie

Jamie is our 4 months old puppy and our newest member. She's very clingy and loves to be around people, so if you want to take good care of someone who will in return give you only love, Jamie is the pup for you!
